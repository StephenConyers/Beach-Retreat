Getting Around

Good bus routes – regular service to Westwood Cross Shopping Centre, open top route 69 sightseeing bus between Ramsgate and Stone Bay available between May and September.

Broadstairs train station is 5 minutes’ walk away – only 5 minutes to Margate and 6 minutes to Ramsgate. Regular services from/to London Victoria, with a fast service from/to London St Pancras
