Rascal Bay Adventure Golf - Manston

holes of outdoor Adventure Golf fun the whole family will love! Featuring pirates, volcanos, rafts, waterfalls and much more.

The Bank Job

Bank Robbery Themed Escape Room based at The Counting House Bar on Broadstairs High Street. As this building was formerly an old Bank, this is about as authentic as you're going to get without doing time!.

Ramsgate Tunnels

The UK's largest network of civilian wartime tunnels.

Dickens House Museum

Based in the cottage that was Charles Dickens' inspiration for the home of Betsey Trotwood in David Copperfield, the museum contains a wide collection of Dickens commemorative items.

The Viking Coastal Trail

32 miles of walking and cycling trails through rural villages and along the scenic Kent coastline.

Dreamland - Margate

There's plenty to do at this 100 year old theme park with its classic rides including the Grade II* listed Scenic Railway, the UK's oldest wooden rollercoaster. Keep an eye out for special music events.

Surf school at Viking Bay

Kent's leading surf and stand-up paddle boarding school based in Viking Bay, Broadstairs.

Lost Island Adventure Indoor Golf

Indoor crazy golf experience you will never forget. Explore the mysterious island whilst you putt your way through dense jungle, secret shack, ferocious dinosaur, fire tiki and more.

Margate Caves

Originally dug as a chalk mine in the 18th century, the Caves have been welcoming visitors since 1863.

Turner Contemporary

Turner Contemporary is one of the UK's leading art galleries. Founded to celebrate JMW Turner's connection to Margate, its iconic building features on the new £20 note. Entry to the gallery and exhibitions is free.

Revolution Skatepark and Climbing Centre

Indoor skatepark and climbing centre catering for all ages and abilities, with a spectators viewing area, snack bar and fully stocked skate shop.

Crampton Tower Museum

Housed in a flint tower that formed part of the first Broadstairs public water supply, built by local engineer Thomas Crampton, it contains Crampton's working drawings, models and graphics.

Wingham Wildlife Park

Kent's fastest growing Wildlife Park housing the largest number of animal species in the county, including tigers, bats and the adorable red panda. There's even a dinosaur zoo!.

Lillyputt Mini Golf

A championship designed mini-golf course complete with a tea garden serving hot and cold food and heaps of ice cream for you to enjoy after your game.

Margate Shell Grotto

Intriguing underground grotto ornately decorated with over 4.6 million shells. Discovered in 1835, its age and purpose remain unknown.

Spitfire & Hurricane Memorial Museum

Situated on the historic RAF airfield at Manston, explore the unique collection of exhibits, including the legendary Spitfire and Hurricane aircraft.
